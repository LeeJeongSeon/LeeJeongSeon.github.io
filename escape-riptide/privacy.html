English

Escape : Riptide ("the app") is a single-player arcade game. We respect your privacy. This policy explains what the app does — and does not — do with your data.

Data collected by us

None. The app has no accounts, no sign-in of our own, and no analytics. We (the developer) do not collect, store, or share any personal information on our own servers — we have none.

Third-party services the app uses

Google AdMob (advertising). The app shows rewarded ads (optional — for example, to revive after sinking) and interstitial ads (shown occasionally between games). To serve ads and prevent fraud, Google may process data such as your device's advertising identifier, IP address, approximate (coarse) location derived from IP, and ad-interaction events. Where required by law (e.g. GDPR regions), a consent form is shown before ads are loaded; if you decline, ads are limited or non-personalized. See Google's privacy policy and how Google uses information from partner apps.

Apple Game Center (leaderboard, optional). If you are signed into Game Center, your best distance is submitted to Apple's leaderboard under your Game Center nickname. This is handled entirely by Apple under Apple's privacy policy; we do not receive or store this data.

Data stored on your device

The app saves your game progress locally on your device only (using the system's standard local storage): best score, collected life-ring (buoy) count, selected and unlocked boat skins, achievement progress, daily-reward status, and settings (sound, vibration, volume, language). This local data is not sent to us and is removed when you delete the app.

Data retention and your choices

We retain no data ourselves. Data processed by Google for advertising is retained and deleted according to Google's data retention policies. Local game data is removed when you delete the app.

Where consent is required by law (e.g. EEA/UK), you can review, change, or withdraw your ad-consent choices at any time in Settings → Ad privacy settings inside the app. On iOS you can additionally limit ad personalization system-wide under Settings → Privacy & Security. For requests regarding data processed by Google, use the controls in Google's privacy policy above; for anything else, email us.

Children

The app is a general-audience game and does not knowingly collect personal data from children. Ads are served through Google AdMob, which supports regulatory settings for child-appropriate treatment.

Changes

If this policy changes, the "Last updated" date above will be revised.

Contact

Questions? Email vneldzpdlzm@gmail.com.
